Hi, my name is Liam Edwards-Playne and on the net I'm known as liamzebedee. I'm a teenager and have been coding since I was 11 (ages ago now). My interests are extremely broad; I spend my time mainly coding, researching P2P networks, learning French and other things at school, reading about startups and business, design and typography, religion, reading dystopian fiction, watching films, and listening to rock music. I also two chronic diseases — type one diabetes and chronic hives.

As someone once said (I adore collecting quotes): Your actions speak louder than your words. I'm not particularly interested in saying stuff about how great I am, I'd rather you form your own opinion of me based on what I've done. The stuff below is as close to a curriculum vitae as I've ever written (the list is not comprehensive), but I'm proud of all these things in some way or another.

To note, the vast majority of things below I am no longer working on. The interests, projects and other things I am currently occupied with are located on the current endeavours page.
